About the project!

This project was created by the "Bathroom Squat(d)", consisting of Cindy Huynh, Brianna Jimenez, and Jenna Liu. In a course of one week, we wrote out the css, js, and html of the webpage. The buttons redirect you to bathrooms in or near SOMA. The "add a bathroom" feature allows for rapid expansion of the website without further coding on our part, and was made possible thanks to databasing in javascript.
